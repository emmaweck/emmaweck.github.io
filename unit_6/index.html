About

Emma Weckerling is a budding marketing professional and blogger living in Washington, D.C. She is currently working as the Digital Asset Coordinator at Brand USA, the nation's first global marketing effort to promote the United States as a premier travel destination.


As the Digital Asset Coordinator, she provides assets for social media, brand campaigns, journalists and travel industry partners. Emma also supports Digital Content Marketing team with creating and updating VisitTheUSA.com web content. With a keen eye for photography, she is responsible for preforming quality assurance to ensure all assets meet creative guidelines as well as trouble-shoot potential issues.


Emma also has solid experience working in the communications industry. This past spring, she completed an internship at Edelman, a global public relations firm. Her experience at Edelman trained her to work efficiently under pressure, develop strong interpersonal skills, stay organized and be responsive. As part of the Corporate Affairs team, Emma worked with many Fortune 500 and national non-profit clients, frequently drafting pitches and press releases.


Emma understands social media as one of the most influential and successful forms of marketing and market research. She considers it a passion and enjoys experimenting with alternative platforms and methods to attempt to better reach new audiences in her professional life as well as in her personal life.


Emma has a great passion for traveling. As a graduate of the University of St. Andrews in Scotland, she was presented with many amazing opportunities to travel the world and has now visited over 20 countries. She also is an enthusiastic cook. To combine these two passions, Emma launched her own website, BakedByEmma.com where she showcases her photography skills, favorite recipes and recent travel adventures.


In her spare time, Emma is currently studying web design. She is enrolled in General Assembly's 10-week intensive HTML, CSS and Web Design course where she is learning to code responsive websites and emails from scratch.
