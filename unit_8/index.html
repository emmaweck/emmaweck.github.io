About

Meet Emma:

Emma Weckerling is a budding marketing professional, aspiring web designer and blogger from Washington, D.C. She is currently working at Brand USA, destination marketing organization for the United States.

On a daily basis, Emma provides assets for social media, brand campaigns, journalists and travel industry partners. She also supports the digital marketing team with creating and updating web content for VisitTheUSA.com. With a keen eye for photography, she is responsible for preforming quality assurance to ensure all assets meet creative guidelines.

Emma also has solid experience working in the communications industry. She completed a 6-month internship at Edelman Public Relations. Her experience at Edelman trained her to work efficiently under pressure, develop strong interpersonal skills, stay organized and be responsive. As part of the Corporate Affairs team, Emma worked with many Fortune 500 and national non-profit clients, frequently drafting pitches and press releases.

Emma understands social media as one of the most influential and successful forms of marketing and market research. She considers it a passion and enjoys experimenting with alternative platforms and methods to attempt to better reach new audiences in her professional life as well as in her personal life.

Emma has a great passion for traveling. As a graduate of the University of St. Andrews in Scotland, she was presented with many amazing opportunities to travel the world and has now visited over 20 countries. She also is an enthusiastic cook. To combine these two passions, Emma launched her own website, BakedByEmma.com where she showcases her photography skills, favorite recipes and recent travel adventures.

In her spare time, Emma is currently studying web design. She is enrolled in General Assembly's 10-week intensive HTML, CSS and Web Design course where she is learning to code responsive websites and emails from scratch.
